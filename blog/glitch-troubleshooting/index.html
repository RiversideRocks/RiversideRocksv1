Common Glitch Errors and How to Fix them.



Glitch is one of my favorite tools online. They let you create a website for free with a realtime editor! Amazing, right? I have been on the Glitch forums quite a bit recently and I would love to share some of the most common issues that I have seen.

1. My project(s) got banned/suspended!
Don't worry! Send an email to support@glitch.com and they will take care of your problem.

2. My discord bot returns an HTTP 429 or Something took to long error.
It appears that your bot was IP banned from discord. Reply to the Discord ban megathread and your project's IP will be changed.

3. I ran out of disk space or memory.
Contact support@glitch.com and they will give you 24 hours of extra space to clear off the extra files.

4. Nothing is loading/I found a Glitch bug
Contact support@glitch.com and let them know!
