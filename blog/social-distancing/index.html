Social Distancing



Recently the Coronavirus, or COVID-19, has swept the world by spreading from person to person. One of the ways we can stop the spread is “social distancing”. Social distancing is the act of avoiding as much contact as possible with other humans. According to Wikipedia social distancing’s objective is “...to reduce the probability of contact between persons carrying an infection, and others who are not infected, so as to minimize disease transmission, morbidity and ultimately, mortality.” So how can we achieve social distancing and protect others and ourselves from disease? Here are a few of the best ways:

- Do not touch your face
- Throw away anything that touches your mouth
- Stay at least three feet away from others while working
- Stay at least six feet away from others
- Avoid large events
- When ordering food, get takeout

If we all work together to achieve social distancing, the virus will slow and life will slowly return back to normal. In other words, don't leave your house unless it is absolutly necessary! Good luck!
