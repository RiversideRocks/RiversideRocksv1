List of Tucker Terms


  • Flag - Tail
  • Yuft - Hair on top of head
  • Barrel - The round end of tucker.
  • Lateral Flap - The chunk of fat near tucker's neck.
  • Dorsal flap - The chunk of fat on Tucker's back
  • P.A.W.S. - The paws are called Personal Automatted Walking System.
  • Gearbox - Tucker's ears
  • BioWaste - Solid waste emitted from an area near the barrel.
  • Cork - The curve in a dog's tail - A name for a bad dog.
  • Auto Splay - When tucker is on his back and both of his legs are spread wide.
  • Skinny Splay - When tucker is on his back and his legs are not spread wide.
  • Fox - When a dog is on its back
  • Cow - When a dog is on its side
  • Sheep - When a dog is not standing but in the upright position.
  • Lazy man's scratch - When a dog scratches its elbow
  • Sato scratch - When a dog scratches its neck
  • Sato Script - The coding language that builds a dog.
  • Core processor - Tucker's brain.
  • Guy - The most common nickname for tucker.
  • Recharging - When Tucker rolls around on the ground to gain more energy.
  • Undercarriage - A dog's stomach
  • Kicker - The leg that gives tucker a good scratch
  • Shift-locking - When a dog moves foreward, but moves its legs sideways
